Privacy Policy for The Pro Lawn (Green Ridge Landscaping, LLC)

Effective Date: February 1, 2024

Welcome to The Pro Lawn! This Privacy Policy describes how Green Ridge Landscaping, LLC, doing business as "The Pro Lawn" ("The Pro Lawn" or "we"), collects, uses, and discloses information about you when you use our website located at www.theprolawn.com (the "Website") and our lawn care services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information about you:

  • Personal Information: When you request a quote, schedule a service, or create an account on the Website, we may collect your name, email address, phone number, address, billing information, and other personal information.
  • Usage Information: We may collect information about your use of the Website, such as the pages you visit, the services you request, and your IP address.
  • Device Information: We may collect information about the device you use to access the Website, such as your browser type, operating system, and device identifier.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To provide you with the lawn care services you request.
  • To communicate with you about your account, services, and promotions.
  • To improve the Website and our services.
  • To personalize your experience on the Website.
  • To comply with legal requirements.

3. Disclosure of Your Information

We may disclose your information to the following third parties:

  • Service providers who help us operate the Website and provide our services, such as payment processors and lawn care professionals.
  • Government agencies or law enforcement officials as required by law.
  • In the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, we may disclose your information to the acquiring entity.

We will not sell your personal information to any third party.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We may use cookies and other tracking technologies to collect information about your use of the Website. Cookies are small files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but please be aware that this may limit your ability to use certain features of the Website.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no website or electronic storage system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee the security of your information.

6. Your Choices

You can access, update, or delete your personal information by logging into your account on the Website. You can also opt out of receiving marketing emails from us by clicking on the "unsubscribe" link in any marketing email we send you.

7. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you believe that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

8. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on the Website. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at info@theprolawn.com
